Answers to frequently asked questions below:

What's the dress code?

Gentlemen: Dressy casual in fall colours. Jackets required, neckwear optional. Wear leather shoes.
Ladies: Something fancier than a sundress but less fancy than a ball gown in fall colours.

What's for dinner?

The food will be from the Elderwood Restaurant. We will send a menu and request your preferences before the event.

Where is Visalia?

Visalia is located 45 minutes south of Fresno, which is serviced by FAT (the airport).

Alternatively, another major airport is LAX, which is 197 miles away, or about a 3 hour drive.

For a slightly longer road trip, SFO is 229 miles away and about a 3 and a half hour drive.

If you don't drive, let us know and we'll try to arrange a ride.

Where can I stay?

We're getting married in a hotel, so the easiest option is to just reserve a room. Room rates are as follows:

Room Price
Queen Guestrooms $179/night
King Guestrooms $209/night
Double Queen Guestroom $269/night
King Suites $329/night
You can also rent an airbnb in Visalia as a cheaper option.

Do I get a plus one?

If you are married, bring your spouse. If you aren't, you're allowed to bring one person.

Are kids allowed?

Yes, but we're not responsible for your unattended children.

Should I bring a gift?

Please don't. There is no gift registry and we won't be accepting any boxed gifts. If you must bring something, either give us money in a red envelope or a thoughtfully written card.

How do I RSVP?

Please tell us if you're coming and if you're bringing a plus one by November 1st, 2022. If you don't reply by then, we're assuming you're not coming and there won't be a seat or a meal for you. You can RSVP by filling out the form in this link.
